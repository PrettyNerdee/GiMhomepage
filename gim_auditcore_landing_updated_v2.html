FAQ

Will this work with our POS?
Yes. If your system can export transactions to CSV or XLSX (e.g., Aztec, ICRTouch, Square, Micros reports), we can analyze it.

No. You export files; we do the rest. Optional check-ins are done over email or a quick call.
What does “read-only” mean?
We never connect to or operate your tills. We only read exported data you provide.
How fast can we start?
Within 48 hours of receiving your first export batch.
What if results aren’t impressive?
We’ll be candid. If we can’t find meaningful opportunities, we’ll tell you plainly and won’t push further services.
