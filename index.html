FAQ

Venue questions, straight answers

What is the Data Health Check?
The Health Check is our mandatory first step. You provide four weeks of POS and roster files (CSV/XLS only), and we produce a Data Quality (DQ) report. It tells you if your data is complete, consistent, and clean enough for a full audit.
Do you handle old data?
Yes. Our Legacy Data Cleanup service is a one-off add-on that processes your historic files into a single, searchable archive. It’s ideal for spotting supplier creep, seasonal trends, or preparing for compliance checks. Starting from £1,000 depending on scope.
Will this work with our POS?
Yes, as long as your system can export line-item sales, shifts, and Z-reads to CSV or XLSX format. We do not support scanned PDFs or photos of reports in our standard cycles.
Is this a subscription?
No. Our model is based on discrete “Cycles.” The Standing Cycle gives you the benefits of a recurring service (a reserved slot and lower price) without the commitment of a subscription.
